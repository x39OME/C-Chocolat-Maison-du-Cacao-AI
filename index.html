01 · Our Gelato

Italian taste, preserved obsessed over, perfected.

Chocolat artisanal gelato is handmade fresh every morning by our gelato makers, using premium ingredients sourced from the same producers we've trusted for two decades.

Our recipes were not invented — they were uncovered. After years of work and research, we preserve the soul of traditional Italian flavors and quietly push them forward.

03 · Inside the Atelier

From bean to spoon — seven slow steps.

01

Sourcing

We travel to Madagascar, Ecuador and Vietnam to taste cacao at the farm.

02

Roasting

Slow, low-temperature roasts that protect each origin's character.

03

Grinding

Stone mills turn nibs into liquor — silent and patient.

04

Conching

72 hours of refinement. Bitterness fades, aromas bloom.

05

Tempering

Crystals aligned by hand on cold marble. Snap, gloss, melt.

06

Moulding

Each tavoletta poured into hand-cut molds, set overnight.

07

Tasting

Nothing leaves the atelier without a final spoon — and a smile.
